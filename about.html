About

Plot and Potion

Plot and Potion is an ongoing portfolio project, featuring: interactive tools, games, and stories. A place for me to experiment with web design and development, and share my works in progress. The name came from an evolved idea for a space to create interactive stories where choices mattered. I love writing and have always enjoyed games where your choices changed the environment around you. I started with simple python code and realized that I had so many more ideas once I began to realize what all could be achieved with some patience and dedication. I was going to need a space to put all the projects and thus, Plot and Potion in it's current state was born.
